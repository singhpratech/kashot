Built for speed, sized to disappear

Hit a hotkey. Drag a region. Annotate. Save, copy, or pin to screen. Get back to work.

Tray-resident, no main window

Lives in your system tray. One global hotkey (default PrintScreen) pulls up the capture overlay across all monitors.

9 annotation tools

Pen, line, arrow, rectangle, ellipse, marker, text, numbered steps, and pixelate / blur. Single-key shortcuts on every tool. Undo / redo always one stroke away.

4 color palettes

Vivid, Highlighter, Pastel, Pro. 16 swatches each, plus a custom color picker. Cycle thickness with one click — your choices persist across sessions.

Pixel-accurate selection

Live magnifier with 4× zoom and pixel coordinates. Drag any edge or corner to resize. Hold Alt and drag to reposition the whole selection.

Save · Copy · Pin

Save as PNG / JPG / BMP, copy straight to clipboard, or pin a borderless top-most window anywhere on your desktop. Optional watermark on saved images.

Lightweight, native, free

~10 MB native binary on Linux and macOS. ~30 MB self-contained on Windows. No Electron. No Wine. No accounts, no telemetry, no upsell. Open source on GitHub.
